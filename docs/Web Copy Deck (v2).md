# Buddas Hawaiian – Web Copy Deck (v2)

> **File:** `copy-deck.md`  
> **Scope:** Core marketing copy for Buddas Hawaiian website  
> **Brand:** Buddas Hawaiian Bakery & Grill – Bring Aloha to the Table

---

## 1. Brand Voice & Tone

### 1.1 Voice

- **Warm & welcoming** – like a regular who knows the staff by name.
- **Confident but not corporate** – we’re polished, but still local.
- **Food-forward** – always bring it back to flavor, comfort, and freshness.
- **Community-minded** – Benefit Nights, local events, families, and teams.

### 1.2 Tone Guidelines

- **Short, punchy headlines**; keep details in subheads and body.
- Write for **mobile readers first** – short sentences and skimmable lists.
- Avoid jargon or overly “chain-y” language.
- Use first-person plural when it feels natural: “we”, “our kitchen”, “our crew”.

---

## 2. Global Elements

### 2.1 Brand Name

- Primary: **Buddas Hawaiian Bakery & Grill**
- Short: **Buddas Hawaiian** (acceptable in headings and nav)

### 2.2 Tagline

- **Bring Aloha to the Table.**

Optional supporting line:

- **Hawaiian-inspired comfort food, fresh-baked rolls, and plates made to share.**

### 2.3 Value Pillars (Short Form)

Use across About, Home, and promotions.

1. **Flavor First** – Big, craveable plates with Hawaiian-inspired comfort.
2. **Everyday Aloha** – Service with warmth, generosity, and care.
3. **Community at the Center** – Benefit Nights, local families, and teams.
4. **Fresh-Baked Goodness** – Budda rolls and bakery items baked with love.
